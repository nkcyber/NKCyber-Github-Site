Our Branches

Branches

NKCyber is an organization for students who are passionate about computer security. Beyond classroom learning, students get together frequently to learn how malicious attackers utilize software, network vulnerabilities to get into computer networks, how to harden computer systems, dissect malicious code and how to defend against intrusions.

Cyber Security Training

Discover cyber security with our CST program. Led by students, for students, our immersive training includes defense tactics, red team skills, and more. Join us to unveil the exciting realm of cyber security.

Research and Development

Discover the junction of coding and cybersecurity in R&D. Join a close-knit community that sparks innovation through impactful projects. Step into the realm of coding, unlocking your cyber potential.

Build Team

Get hands-on with infrastructure, networking, and DevOps in the Build Team. We keep things running and teach secure server setups. Join our close community for learning and cyber empowerment.

Cyber Security Training

Dive into the world of cyber security with our student-led Cyber Security Training (CST). Explore a spectrum of topics, from defense strategies to red team exercises. Ideal for passionate students, our CST community bridges expertise across generations, fostering a dynamic environment where learning and fun converge. Embark on an exciting journey that demystifies cyber security while creating lasting connections with like-minded peers.

Research and Development

Unleash your potential within our R&D branch, where computer science and cyber security intertwine seamlessly. We drive innovation through impactful projects that enrich both the club and its members. Join our close-knit community of collaborators, where the fusion of sharing knowledge and honing coding prowess come together effortlessly. Discover the transformative power of coding with us.

Build Team

Embark on infrastructure, networking, and DevOps journey with the Build Team. Ensuring seamless operations, teaching secure server setups, and fostering hands-on learning. Join our community for empowerment and camaraderie, unveiling the backbone of our cyber journey. Here, practical expertise and collaborative spirit fuel your tech aspirations.
